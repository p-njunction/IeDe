Who We Are

IeDe Technologies is a forward-thinking innovation partner. We don't just build software; we engineer intelligent ecosystems. From embedded hardware to cloud-scale AI, we bridge the physical and digital worlds to create seamless, impactful solutions for businesses and consumers.

IeDe Technologies, established in 1997, is a global software solutions and product development company dedicated to providing expert software development services and solutions for small, medium and large companies. We operate with the primary mission of providing the most effective solutions to our customers and play a pivotal role in their growth and success. We give customers the flexibility of onsite and offshore outsourcing with frameworks and IP enhanced approach to fulfill their needs in the areas of software and systems product development, systems integration, application development, software re-engineering and engineering services.
